Introducing XTouch DJ

The Fate of Music is at your Fingertips

Simple User Interface and Design

The XTouch Tap DJ is simple to use and simple to navigate. Users select screen elements, and then assign it to a table spot by tapping anywhere on the surface on that table. When all your intruments and table spots are configured, simple trigger the record button and start making music!
